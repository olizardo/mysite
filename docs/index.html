Omar Lizardo

I am the LeRoy Neiman Term Chair Professor of Sociology at the University of California, Los Angeles.

I study culture, cognition, networks, theory, consumption, institutions, and organization.

PhD in Sociology, University of Arizona, 2006.

BS in Psychology, Brooklyn College of the City University of New York, 1997.

I am also:

  • Former co-editor of American Sociological Review.
  • Former chair of the American Sociology Association’s Section on Sociology of Culture.
  • Current member of the Sociological Research Association.
  • Current member of the Board of Reviewing Editors of the journal Science.
  • Associate editor for the journal Discover Data.
  • Current member of the Editorial Advisory Board of the journals Poetics, and Journal for the Theory of Social Behaviour
    • and the book series Stanford University Press Series on Culture and Economic Life and Princeton University Press Series on Studies in Cultural Sociology.
  • Current co-editor of the journal Sociological Theory, with Z. Magubane, V. Patil and S. L. Crawley.
